Unique Siddha Oracle 1008 by Jivanmukti Inspiration by Contemplation

After many years of voluntary meditation and practices, and futile visits to self-proclaimed gurus, most of the people come to the conclusion that they still do not know what meditation or yoga or samadhi are, and how to actually meditate?

It is so because most of the people lack the direct experience. Most of the teachings in the modern spiritual world are dead because they are devoid of transmission power and direct experience of yoga and meditation – devoid of stillness, simplicity & selflessness. That is why, such teachings feed people with promises, bookish knowledge, futile practices & empty talks. The majority of the people in the field of spirituality still believe that “Samadhi” is an achievement of enlightenment and “Yoga” is about physical exercises and control of the breath.

Siddha Oracle is a portal that takes one from a limited perspective of thought-based knowledge and information to a limitless space of spontaneous expression, vision and higher guidance. Siddha Oracle is a tool for connecting with one’s own essence and the flow of one’s essential being – the life itself. Siddha Oracle will not serve one as a tool for finding out about the lives of others or for predicting the future. Therefore, one should only ask questions that pertain to oneself and one’s own life and journey.

Siddha Oracle 1008 is a Living Siddha Transmission created by Jivanmukti. By deepening one’s connection with the Oracle, one is going to deepen the connection with themselves and the Ultimate. Siddha Oracle is a unique tool for spiritual contemplation & self-refinement. Contemplation with Siddha Oracle is going to expand your awareness, support you through the process of spiritual awakening and self-refinement, and offer a profound insight into the emotional & spiritual layers of your being as well as the path to overcome your current evolutionary obstacles.
