Meet Jivanmukti

Shaktipat Stream is the stream of on-going programs offered by Jivanmukti and Siddhanta Yoga Academy that are available to Shaktipat Stream supporters for an all-inclusive (excluding retreats and fundraising events) monthly gratitude donation. Membership grants free access to powerful spiritual transmissions, deep meditative experience & initiation into the depths of Siddha Teachings, unique webinars, special blessings & meditation events, individual learning and one-on-one guidance.Shaktipat Stream is intended for serious adepts who are already familiar with the teaching through Jivanmukti’s books, programs and sessions, and who feel a strong inner call to immerse in the teaching in a more intense and transformative way and to selflessly integrate the teaching in one’s being and life.

If you are familiar with Jivanmukti´s teachings through her books, programs and sessions and feel a pulll to learn with Jivanmukti and experience the teaching within the self you may support the path and join Shaktipat Stream for a monthly gratitude donation.
