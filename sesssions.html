About Siddha Insight with Jivanmukti

Through the sessions with Jivanmukti, one is immersed into a powerful spiritual presence that emanates direct experience of Yoga – meditative absorption – awakening to the Ultimate Reality of Self. The Consciousness and the Presence of Jivanmukti carry a seed of awakening or light-body activation and touch anyone who experiences it in-person or on a distance, the process of which gradually unfolds over time. The essence of this meeting will keep unfolding throughout your life through your own conscious connection with Siddha Being, revealing itself as one’s limitations dissolve the layers of the limited, illusive self, and the light of the authentic, natural and pure consciousness re-emerges. These sessions are a spontaneous flow of wisdom, knowledge and personal revelation, where one will see by reflection various distorted layers of one’s own being, subtle obstructions and ‘psycho-spiritual’ misalignments that prevent one from realising the freedom within the self that unleashes the dormant creative potential to build a harmonious life full of abundance, self-born knowledge and harmony, laying the foundation for a new experience and further evolution.
